Bentley Continental GT

A stunning showcase of Bentley's unrivaled attention to detail, the Continental GT combines spirited, focused performance with handcrafted luxury and cutting-edge technology, creating the finest Grand Tourer ever produced.

Legendary Craftsmanship

Every Bentley Continental GT is handcrafted in Crewe, England, where our dedicated team of artisans spend over 100 hours on each vehicle. The interior alone contains almost ten square meters of wood veneer, applied by hand and mirror-matched to create a perfectly symmetrical pattern.

The leather hides are sourced from Northern European bulls that graze at high altitude, where there are fewer insects to mark their skin. Each Continental GT uses up to 10 premium hides, each one inspected by hand and selected for quality and consistency of color.

Features & Highlights

Performance

The Continental GT is powered by an enhanced version of Bentley's renowned 6.0-liter W12 engine, delivering 626 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque. The result is breathtaking acceleration and a top speed of 207 mph, making it one of the fastest grand tourers in the world.

Design

The Continental GT's design is characterized by its muscular proportions and elegant lines. The aluminum exterior panels are superformed using aerospace techniques, allowing for complex shapes and sharper lines. The car sits lower and wider than its predecessor, with a more dynamic stance.

Interior

Step inside the Continental GT to discover a world of unparalleled luxury. The cabin features a unique "dual personality" design with the latest technology seamlessly integrated with the finest handcrafted materials. Over 10 square meters of sustainably sourced wood veneer are mirror-matched by hand to create perfect symmetry.

Technology

The Continental GT combines traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology. The highlight is the Bentley Rotating Display, a three-sided rotating dashboard feature that allows you to choose between a 12.3-inch touchscreen, three elegant analog dials, or a clean, veneer-only surface.

The Bentley Rotating Display

A unique feature of the Continental GT is the innovative Bentley Rotating Display. This elegant and sophisticated feature epitomizes the exquisite craftsmanship and engineering that go into the Continental GT.

At first glance, the centerpiece of the dashboard appears to be a seamless piece of veneer. But at the push of a button, it rotates to reveal a 12.3-inch touchscreen or three elegant analog dials: an outside temperature gauge, a compass, and a chronometer.
