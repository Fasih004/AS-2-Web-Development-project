Features & Highlights

Performance

The heart of the DB12 is a twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V8 engine, producing 671 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. This formidable powertrain propels the DB12 from 0-60 mph in just 3.6 seconds and delivers a top speed of 202 mph.

Design

The DB12's design is an evolution of Aston Martin's iconic styling with a more muscular stance. It features a larger grille, sculpted sides, and a redesigned rear end with integrated tailpipes. Every line and curve serves a purpose, blending aesthetics with aerodynamic efficiency.

Interior

The cabin of the DB12 showcases Aston Martin's commitment to luxury. Hand-stitched Bridge of Weir leather, real metal, and carbon fiber elements create an atmosphere of sophisticated comfort. The seating positions have been optimized to enhance comfort during extended drives.

Technology

The DB12 introduces Aston Martin's next-generation infotainment system with a 10.25-inch touchscreen, wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, and a premium 11-speaker sound system. Advanced driver assistance systems include adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and automatic emergency braking.
